The point of this exercise is to use JavaScript to display and hide dropdown menus. When a menu button is clicked, the max-height of the div containing all its options is toggled between zero and the sum of the offset height of its child elements. With a transition time of .2s and the overflow set to hidden, this makes menu options smoothly wipe from the bottom of its respective button.
